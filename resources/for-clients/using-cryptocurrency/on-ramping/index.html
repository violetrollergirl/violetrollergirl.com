On-ramping to Cryptocurrency (Reference Sheet)

Summary of recommendations:

  • if you can wait a week, buy ZEC with ACH on Gemini (~1.5% fee), then send to your self-custodial wallet in a week.
  • if you need it today, buy BTC on CashApp (~1.8% fee), then use NEAR Intents to swap to get the ZEC equivalent in your wallet.
