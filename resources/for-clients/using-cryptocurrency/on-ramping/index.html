On-ramping to Cryptocurrency via Exchanges (Reference Sheet)

Summary of recommendations:

  • if you can wait a week, buy ZEC with ACH on Gemini (~1.5% fee), then send to your provider or your (self-custodial) wallet in a week.
  • if you need it today, buy BTC on CashApp (~2% fee), then send to your provider or perform a swap deposit to get the ZEC equivalent in your wallet.
