It's okay, you can always cancel or reschedule a date for any reason, no questions asked. If you cancel with:

  • 3 or more days (72 hours) notice, the full amount (100%) of your deposit can be applied toward the fee for another date.
  • 3 to 1 days (72-24 hours) notice, half (50%) of your deposit can be applied one time only toward the fee for another date.
  • 1 day or less (24 hours) notice, your deposit will be forfeited. I also consider ghosting to be a cancellation.

When applying the deposit for a cancelled date towards my fee for the next, that next date must be no later than 3 months after your original booking. Past deposits only apply towards the next date's fee, not their initial deposits; deposits are unconditionally required to reserve any time in my calendar.

In the rare event I'm forced to cancel our date, I will offer to promptly return your full deposit to you and will make every effort to give us another opportunity to meet.
